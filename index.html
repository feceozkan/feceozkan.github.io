I am a Ph.D. student in Psychology at the University of Toronto, working with Dr. Samuel Ronfard in the Childhood Learning and Development Lab. I received my M.A. in Psychology at Koç University, where I worked with Dr. Aylin Küntay in the Language and Communication Development Lab. I got my B.Sc. in Psychology at METU, where I also completed a minor degree in Logic and Philosophy of Science.


My research projects focus on the development of human social learning and reasoning: how children and adults search, evaluate, and talk about information and information sources and how they form and revise their beliefs.
